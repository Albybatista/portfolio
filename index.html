About Me

Hello, there!
        I'm Alby and I'm a creative, solutions-oriented software developer. I'm looking to combine my experiences with people in sales, business, and software development in order to maximize overall company efficiency. Being a great communicator and having a people first mentality has paved the way for most of my successes. This outlook has allowed me to think like a user, the experience, and finally, as a business. Currently, what I enjoy most as a developer is the ability to have a hands on approach to how things work and how they can be improved.
      As a developer, my favorite place is a blank text editor. Being a kinesthetic learner, all I’ve known from a young age has been how to build and break in order to recreate. One of the most gratifying feelings is coming up with a creative solution to a problem while collaborating with others. My foundation at General Assembly’s bootcamp has allowed me to mesh both new and dormant passions in a multitude of ways.
